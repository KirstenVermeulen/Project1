The Smart Locker

Get your post faster & easier

SmartPost is a innovative product to enhance the way you get your postal packages. The intention of this prototype is to optimize the accessability by automizing the process which delivers your package. A big benefit of the smart locker is that it is good for the nature. By collecting all packages in one place, postmen don't have to drive as much as they do now. So think of mother nature and choose for SmartPost!
